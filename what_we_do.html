What We Do

ACSS

To create a collaborative learning environment where University of the East students can come together to share knowledge, skills, and experiences related to computer studies and systems.

To promote the professional development and career opportunities of University of the East students in the field of computer studies and systems.

PURPOSE

Goals

One of the goals of the organization is to cultivate a community of students who harbor interests in the realm of technology, its innovative advancements, and diverse applications. Also, to organize events specialized at assisting Computer Studies students in igniting their enthusiasm in technology and connecting them with peers across various academic years.

Our Advocacy

To organize events, workshops, and fun activities where you can learn new skills, make friends, and maybe even land that dream tech job after graduation.

To host quiz bees, coding competitions, and tech challenges throughout the year. These events give you the chance to put your skills to the test, collaborate with teammates, and come up with innovative solutions to real-world problems. Plus, they are a great way to beef up your resume and impress future employers.

To manage social events like game tournaments, movie marathons, and networking mixers.

To have some fun while diving deep into the world of computer studies!
